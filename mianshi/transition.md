# 🎭 场景1：支持型经理（最理想）

---

## 🧑‍💼 Manager：

> Hey Fei, what did you want to chat about?

---

## 🗣️ 你：

> Thanks for taking the time. I wanted to share that I’ve accepted an internal opportunity within TD.

---

## 🧑‍💼 Manager：

> Oh nice — congrats! What role is it?

---

## 🗣️ 你：

> It’s a DevOps-focused role with more emphasis on CI/CD and platform engineering, which aligns closely with where I want to grow long term.

---

## 🧑‍💼 Manager：

> That sounds like a great move.

---

## 🗣️ 你：

> Thanks, I really appreciate your support. I’ve learned a lot on this team.

---

## 🧑‍💼 Manager：

> When are you planning to move?

---

## 🗣️ 你：

> I aligned for about a 3-week transition period so I can wrap up or hand over my current work properly.

---

## 🧑‍💼 Manager：

> That’s helpful, thanks.

---

## 🗣️ 你（收尾）：

> I’ll make sure everything is documented and transitioned smoothly — happy to support however needed.

---

# 🎯 这个场景目标：

👉 维持关系 + 建立好口碑（非常成功）

---

# 🎭 场景2：有点失望但理性

---

## 🧑‍💼 Manager：

> Oh… I wasn’t aware you were looking.

---

## 🗣️ 你：

> I understand. I wanted to make sure things were confirmed before bringing it up. I really value your support and everything I’ve learned here.

---

## 🧑‍💼 Manager：

> What made you decide to move?

---

## 🗣️ 你（关键🔥）：

> I’ve really enjoyed working on this team. This opportunity just aligns more closely with my long-term focus on DevOps and platform engineering, so I felt it was the right next step.

---

## 🧑‍💼 Manager：

> I see… what about your current project?

---

## 🗣️ 你：

> I’ve been thinking about that — I can either complete the key parts over the next few weeks or prepare a proper handover so someone else can pick it up smoothly.

---

## 🧑‍💼 Manager：

> Okay, we’ll need to plan that.

---

## 🗣️ 你（收尾）：

> Absolutely — I’ll make sure the transition is smooth and support however needed.

---

# 🎯 这个场景目标：

👉 降低情绪 + 转移到“解决问题”

---

# 🎭 场景3：偏不开心 / 被动防御型

---

## 🧑‍💼 Manager：

> I wish I had known earlier. We could have looked at opportunities here.

---

## 🗣️ 你（关键话术🔥）：

> I understand, and I really appreciate that. I wanted to make sure everything was confirmed before bringing it up, and I truly value your support.

---

## 🧑‍💼 Manager：

> So you’ve already accepted?

---

## 🗣️ 你：

> Yes, I’ve accepted the offer.

---

## 🧑‍💼 Manager：

> Hmm… okay. When are you planning to move?

---

## 🗣️ 你：

> I aligned for about a 3-week transition so I can wrap up or hand over work properly.

---

## 🧑‍💼 Manager：

> We’ll need to figure out your responsibilities.

---

## 🗣️ 你：

> Absolutely — I’m happy to document everything and help with knowledge transfer.

---

# 🎯 这个场景目标：

👉 不对抗 + 不解释过多 + 稳住局面

---

# 🎭 场景4：试图留你（关键场景🔥）

---

## 🧑‍💼 Manager：

> Is there anything we can do to keep you?

---

## 🗣️ 你（一定要用这个）：

> I really appreciate that. I’ve thought carefully about it, and I believe this move is the right next step for me.

---

## 🧑‍💼 Manager：

> Is it about compensation or something else?

---

## 🗣️ 你：

> It’s more about the role focus and long-term direction. I want to grow more in platform and DevOps engineering.

---

## 🧑‍💼 Manager：

> We could explore similar opportunities here.

---

## 🗣️ 你（收口🔥）：

> I really appreciate that, but I’ve already committed to this move and I think it’s the right step for me at this point.

---

# 🎯 这个场景目标：

👉 **不被拉回去 + 不伤关系**

---

# 🎭 场景5：强关注项目交付（你这个case很可能遇到）

---

## 🧑‍💼 Manager：

> What about [your project]? That’s still ongoing.

---

## 🗣️ 你（关键🔥）：

> Yes, I’m aware — I’ve been thinking about how to transition it smoothly.
> I can focus on completing the critical parts or prepare documentation and handover so someone else can continue.

---

## 🧑‍💼 Manager：

> We’ll need a plan.

---

## 🗣️ 你：

> Absolutely — I can put together a transition plan and walk the team through it.

---

---

# 🎯 这个场景目标：

👉 让他放心，而不是纠结你走

---

# 🧠 最关键的3个“统一原则”

---

## ✅ 1. 永远不解释“为什么面试”

👉 不需要提

---

## ✅ 2. 永远用这个理由

👉 growth / alignment / next step

---

## ✅ 3. 永远主动讲 transition

👉 这是你最大加分项🔥

---

# ⭐ 最关键一句（所有场景通用）

👉 如果你卡住：

> I believe this move aligns with my long-term growth, and I’ll make sure to support a smooth transition.
